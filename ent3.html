FULFILLING THE ESSENCE OF OPERA.












MISSION

From the drama at the core of each work, Heartbeat Opera grows vivid theatrical worlds through revelatory adaptations, radical rearrangements, and ingenious design. Through an inquisitive collaborative process with a diverse community of artists, we break down traditional barriers to reimagine opera for artists and audiences of the twenty-first century.

WORK

In its first five seasons, Heartbeat Opera has presented eight fully realized productions: Kurtág's Kafka-Fragments, Offenbach's Daphnis & Chloé, Donizetti's Lucia di Lammermoor, Purcell's Dido and Aeneas, Bizet's Carmen, Puccini's Butterfly, Beethoven’s Fidelio, Mozart’s Don Giovanni, and Stradella’s La Susanna. Heartbeat has also staged the first ever opera show on The High Line, and has mounted a Benefit Drag Extravaganza each year since its founding: Dragus Maximus, All The World's A Drag: Shakespeare in Love...With Opera and Queens of the Night: Mozart in Space, The Fairy Queen and Miss Handel. Finally, we've staged workshop productions of Lucia di Lammermoor and Weill's Seven Deadly Sins; and collaborated with organizations such as Atlas DIY and A BroaderWay to bring opera education to underserved youths in the New York City area.
